Gameplay


Game Controls

The most important skill in Bike Unchained 2 gameplay-wise is to follow the track you're riding and anticipate the changes of a trail.

You have intuitive touch-screen controls: to increase your speed on the downhill parts of a trail tap and hold your finger on the screen and release the pump on uphills to maintain the speed.


While this makes super easy game controls, this kind of gameplay requires a lot of skill to choose perfect moments to tap-and-hold and release your finger. Also you will have to explore the tracks in the game thoroughly to progress.

You can learn about locations in the game in this article.

Tricks Gameplay

For the Slopestyle events you can select one of the many available tricks you've learned from your in-game mentors -- real-world Red Bull athletes.

You have to choose tricks you want to perform before the ride:


You are provided with the suggested trick difficulty for all jumps you will do during a given trail.

Tap on all 'Select Trick' icons to choose your tricks. Whenever you choose tricks for all jumps, you are all set to start your Slopestyle ride.


Mentors

The in-game mentors are real-world Red Bull athletes who are integrated into the game to teach your character new tricks and biking skills.

You can review the mentors collection in the game's Mentors tab.

In order to see specific skills, gears and abilities which your character can get from a given mentor, simply tap on a given 'Unlock' icon:

Also, you are welcome to check out and follow social media accounts of your mentors to stay tuned to the latest events in real life biking as well.


Abilities

The in-game abilities are used to boost your character's or bike's characteristics.

For instance, you can improve your speed, stamina, air control, landing, tricks, and/or your bike's rolling resistance just before the start of any given ride to improve your chances of winning it.

You can learn/unlock new abilities whenever you win mentor challenges or reach a new level mentor level.

You can learn more about Mentors in this article.


What are leagues and how do they work?

Once you complete the tutorial of the game, Ranked Race will be available to you to play.

This is where you’ll compete against other riders, and depending of your results, your stand in the league will be determined.

You will start in the Bronze League and the more you play and the more races you win, the bigger your chances to be promoted to the next one. Each promotion gives you new challenges and opportunities to get amazing rewards!


How many leagues are there in the game?

The current leagues in order from lowest to highest are, as follows:

  • Bronze
  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Platinum
  • Diamond
  • Master
  • Grand Master

Why do I need to progress to higher league in the game?

League progression is the game's main objective, as higher leagues provide better rewards for the rides and higher score points for any of the game events.

That is, you can upgrade your bike, customize your character appearance, and complete challenges faster.

In-Game Events


What types of events are there in the game?
There are four main types of events in the game:
Ranked/League events

Ranked/League event is available from the game's "PLAY" menu. This is the most important event for your progression in the game, as by winning it you can move to higher league.

Learn why you need to move to a higher leagues in Why do I need to progress to higher league in the game? article.


Daily event

Daily event is available from the game's "PLAY" menu. The "Daily event" objectives (e.g. slopestyle, etc.) and standings are updated every 24 hours.

That is, it your chance to compete and win rewards every day!

You have three free attempts to show your best score for the event. When you run out of attempts, you need to use tickets for every new ride.


Weekly event

Weekly event is available from the game's "PLAY" menu. The "Weekly event" objectives (e.g. slopestyle, etc.) and standings are updated every week.

That is, it your chance to compete and win BIG TIME every week!

You have three free attempts to show your best score for the event. When you run out of attempts, you need to use tickets for every new ride.


Special event

Special event is available from the game's "PLAY" menu. The "Special event" objectives (e.g. slopestyle, etc.) are determined by the production team. The time frame for the event can vary as well.

Some of the "Special events" have real world rewards for the top players in the leaderboard (e.g. biking shorts, Red Bull T-shirts, etc.).

To learn more about Special events with real world prizes, please check out How can I take part in events with real world prizes? article.

How can I take part in events with real world prizes?

In order to be able to participate, players need to be at least “Gold” league in the game or higher. The current "Real World Prizes" event will be shown on the top of the ‘Play’ section and can be accessed by clicking on it. (e.g. below).


The rules for the events may vary from event to event; however, every "Real World Prizes" event has the "Info" tab which redirects you to the official rules for a given event.

To check out the prizes for a given event, you can simply tap on the "View Prizes" tab from the event menu.

Gold, Obtainium and Payments


Is Bike Unchained 2 free to play?

Bike Unchained 2 is free to install and play. In-game currency can be used to speed up progress; however, all of the game's content can be access and played with or without it.

Also, there are in-game offers, which can be purchased with Obtainium, Gold or real money. Purchases are made through in-game Shop. All the purchases are processed exclusively through iTunes or Google Play services to maintain maximum security.

Obtainium can be obtained in game as reward for participating in the in-game challenges or ranked/daily/weekly/special events.

How to make an in-game purchase?

There are two ways to purchase in-game items (currency, bike upgrades, tickets, etc.) in the game:

Tapping [+] for Tickets, Gold or Obtainium
In the "Shop" section of the game menu
